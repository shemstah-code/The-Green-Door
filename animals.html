How it works

From rescue to home

01
Rescue or intake
An animal is reported, found, or surrendered. We assess the situation and arrange safe pickup or transfer.
02
Vet check and stabilisation
Every animal gets a health check, vaccinations where needed, and treatment for injuries or illness before anything else.
03
Decompression and care
Quiet space, consistent food, gentle handling, and enrichment — including natural chews — help nervous animals settle and build trust.
04
Grooming and preparation
Once an animal is ready, grooming sessions help them look and feel their best before meeting potential adopters.
05
Adoption or foster placement
We match animals with the right home — whether that's a permanent adoption or a foster placement while we continue searching.
