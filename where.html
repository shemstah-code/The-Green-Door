Our base

Home and outreach

The Green Door Jamaica is based in Kingston, but our rescue and care work reaches beyond the city. We respond to reports of animals in need across parishes, coordinate transport, and work with local community members to get animals the help they need.

We focus on keeping operations small enough to do well — a handful of animals at a time, with real attention paid to each one.
